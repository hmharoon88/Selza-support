Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my first location?

1. Open Selza and sign in to your account
2. Tap "Choose Location" on the dashboard
3. Tap "Add New Location"
4. Enter location name, address, and optional PIN
5. Tap "Save Location" to complete setup

How do I scan scratch-off tickets?

1. Open the barcode scanner from the dashboard
2. Point your camera at the ticket barcode
3. The app will automatically detect and process the ticket
4. Ticket information will be displayed and saved
5. You can also use external Bluetooth scanners for faster processing

How do I start tracking a shift?

1. Go to "Current Shift" from the dashboard
2. Tap "Start Shift" to begin tracking
3. The app will automatically track time and sales
4. Tap "End Shift" when you're finished
5. View detailed shift reports in "Last Shift"

How do I manage inventory?

1. Go to "Update Inventory" from the dashboard
2. Add new scratch-off tickets with game numbers
3. Set initial stock levels for each ticket type
4. Scan tickets to automatically update inventory
5. View real-time stock levels and sales data

Can I use Selza offline?

Yes! Selza works offline with local data storage. When you're back online, all data will automatically sync to the cloud. This ensures you never lose important information even without internet connection.

How do I add multiple users?

1. Go to Settings → User Management
2. Tap "Add User"
3. Enter the new user's email address
4. Set appropriate permissions and access levels
5. The user will receive an invitation email

How secure is my data?

Selza uses enterprise-grade security with Firebase authentication and encryption. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never share your business data with third parties.

How do I export my data?

1. Go to Settings → Data Management
2. Tap "Export Data"
3. Choose the date range and data types
4. Select export format (CSV, PDF)
5. Download or email the report
